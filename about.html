Wild Horse All Assam Karate Championship 2025

We are excited to announce the Wild Horse All Assam Karate Championship, an exceptional and prestigious martial arts event that will take place on March 21, 22, and 23, 2025, at the esteemed Dibrugarh University, located in the vibrant and culturally rich city of Dibrugarh. This championship will serve as a platform for elite karate athletes from across Assam, who will compete in a variety of high-level events, showcasing their skills, discipline, and competitive spirit. We are proud to bring this grand event to life and celebrate the art of karate in an environment that is both energizing and inspiring.

Set against the backdrop of Assam’s stunning natural beauty, the Wild Horse All Assam Karate Championship draws direct inspiration from the majestic wild horses of Dibru-Saikhowa National Park, whose spirit of resilience and freedom resonates throughout the competition. Much like these incredible animals that roam the park, our athletes are bound to demonstrate unmatched strength, focus, and perseverance. The event will feature a range of dynamic categories, ensuring that each participant has the opportunity to shine in their respective discipline. The Wild Horse Championship is not just about karate; it’s about celebrating the essence of endurance, determination, and the indomitable human spirit.

As we prepare to host this spectacular event, we aim to create an atmosphere that celebrates the rich cultural heritage of Assam while showcasing the artistry and athleticism of karate. With the breathtaking beauty of Dibrugarh surrounding us, this championship will undoubtedly become a landmark event in the state’s martial arts scene. Visitors will have the chance to witness thrilling competitions while immersing themselves in the culture, tradition, and natural splendor of Assam. Whether you're an athlete, a supporter, or a visitor, this event promises to offer a memorable experience for all who are part of it.

Join us for this remarkable event, as we come together to honor the traditions of karate and the strength of human willpower, set against one of India’s most scenic and ecologically rich locations. The Wild Horse All Assam Karate Championship is not just a competition—it is a celebration of martial arts, culture, and the unyielding power of nature. We invite you to witness the incredible performances, cheer for the athletes, and experience the perfect blend of sport and nature in a truly extraordinary setting. Get ready for an unforgettable experience—be part of this historic event!

Event Dates: March 21-23, 2025

The Wild Horse All Assam Karate Championship 2025 will be held at the prestigious Dibrugarh University, located in the beautiful city of Dibrugarh, Assam.
