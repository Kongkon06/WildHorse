Wild Horse All Assam Karate Championship 2025

We are thrilled to announce the Wild Horse All Assam Karate Championship, a premier martial arts event that will take place on March 21, 22, and 23, 2025, at Dibrugarh University in the vibrant city of Dibrugarh. This championship will bring together karate athletes from across Assam, who will compete in a variety of exciting events and categories.

Event Dates: March 21-23, 2025

The championship will feature the following events:

  • Kata – Individual performance of traditional karate patterns
  • Kumite – Sparring competition
  • Team Kata – Group performance of karate patterns
  • Team Kumite – Team sparring competition

Categories include:

  • Sub Junior – Age group below 10 years
  • Cadet – Age group 11-14 years
  • Junior – Age group 15-17 years
  • Under 21 – Age group 18-21 years
  • Senior – Age group above 21 years

The Wild Horse All Assam Karate Championship 2025 will be held at the prestigious Dibrugarh University, located in the beautiful city of Dibrugarh, Assam.
